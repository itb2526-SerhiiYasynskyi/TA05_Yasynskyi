Cybersecurity reports and threat intelligence

In this section, you will find a curated collection of cybersecurity reports that provide valuable insights into the latest cyber threats, attack techniques, and security trends. These reports are published by leading cybersecurity organizations and research teams and are designed to help professionals, researchers, and students better understand the evolving cyber threat landscape.

The reports cover a wide range of topics, including ransomware, phishing, cloud security, insider threats, vulnerability management, and attack surface analysis. Together, they offer actionable recommendations and real-world data that can be used to improve security strategies and protect digital infrastructures.

Key cybersecurity reports

  • Digital Defense Report — A Microsoft report analyzing modern cyber threats and defensive strategies.
  • Annual Cybersecurity Research — An annual publication with in-depth threat and trend analysis.
  • State of the Phish — Focused research on phishing techniques and protection methods.
  • Huntress SMB Threat Report — Threats affecting small and medium-sized businesses and recommendations.
  • X-Force Cloud Threat Report — IBM X-Force insights into cloud incidents and best practices.
  • X-Force Threat Intelligence Index — Global threat trends and attacker behavior statistics.
  • Threat Monitor — Overview of major cybersecurity incidents and monitoring guidance.
  • M-Trends Special Report — Mandiant’s analysis of attacks, techniques, and defenses.
  • State of Vulnerability Management — Vulnerability identification and remediation practices.
  • Unit 42 Ransomware Report — Ransomware threat analysis and mitigation strategies.
  • Unit 42 Attack Surface Analysis — Guidance to reduce exposure and improve security posture.
  • Cost of Insider Threats — Impact analysis and prevention recommendations.
  • Human Factor in Cybersecurity — Human error, social engineering, and awareness improvements.
  • Cost of a Data Breach — Financial impact of breaches and key cost-driving factors.
  • Cyber Threat Retrospect — Retrospective review of threats, vulnerabilities, and trends.

These reports serve as essential resources for understanding cybersecurity risks and developing effective defense strategies in a complex digital environment.
